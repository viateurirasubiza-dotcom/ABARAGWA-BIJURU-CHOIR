Amateka Yacu

Chorale yacu yashinzwe hagamijwe gufasha abantu kwegera Imana binyuze mu ndirimbo z’ihimbaza n’ivugabutumwa.

Intego (Mission)

Gukwirakwiza ubutumwa bwiza binyuze mu ndirimbo no gufasha abantu kugira ubuzima bw’umwuka buhamye.

Icyerekezo (Vision)

Kuba chorale izwi mu gihugu hose mu kuramya Imana no gukoresha impano mu murimo w’Imana.
